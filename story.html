The Engineer's
Formula.

For years, my world was governed by mathematics, structural integrity, and tangible metrics. As an engineer based in the heart of Silicon Valley, my focus was on building systems that worked flawlessly under pressure. But there is a ceiling to what you can express with pure logic.

Parallax Parfums was born from a desire to apply that same rigorous, technical discipline to something deeply visceral. I realized that a great fragrance is simply invisible architecture. It requires a precise understanding of molecular weights, evaporation curves, and material interactions to create a structure that holds up over time.

We don't just blend scents. We engineer them.

The Art of the
Parallax.

In physics, parallax is the effect where an object's position appears to shift when viewed from different angles. This principle is the foundation of our house. True mastery comes from exploring the extremes.

Every fragrance we formulate belongs to one of two opposing lines of sight:

Engineered for
Endurance.

The most common flaw in modern, mass-market perfumery is structural collapse—fragrances that smell beautiful for an hour, only to fade entirely.

— ENGINEERING VERDICT —
That is a design failure.

At Parallax Parfums, we formulate almost exclusively at Parfum and Extrait concentrations. By using a significantly higher percentage of premium aromatic compounds, we ensure our fragrances don't just possess an incredible opening, but offer exceptional all-day performance, sillage, and absolute value.

A.

High Concentration

20–30%

Aromatic compounds. Two to three times the typical EDP.
