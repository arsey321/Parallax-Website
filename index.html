II The Engineer's Approach

// THE STORY

A great fragrance is
invisible architecture.

Parallax was founded by an engineer. The bench in Santa Clara is run on the same logic as any other — calipers, scales, log books — only what we're measuring is volatility, not torque.

We formulate at parfum concentration (20–30% aromatic compounds, not the 8–15% on the retail shelf), in small batches, by hand. Less ethanol. More intention.
